Problem statement: Electronic waste in the modern world

The many computing devices in the modern world generate a large amount of electronic waste and this has a negative effect on both the environment and the economy.

Affinity diagram: Electronic waste ideas

This diagram is split into 5 categories containing ideas and concerns on how to prevent and lessen electronic waste in the world and the potential consequences of those ideas. It is one big "brainstorm" that our group hopes will lead to a definite solution on how to lessen the wastefulness of electronics and in turn make a positive impact on climate change and the economy.
